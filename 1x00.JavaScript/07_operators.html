Operators

We have a few different types of operators

  1. Arithmetic Operators

    • Addition

      10 + 10

    • Subtraction

      10 - 10

    • Multiplication

      10 * 10

    • Division

      10 / 10

    • Modulus

      10 % 10

    • Exponent

      2 ** 4

    • Concatenation

      'First' + ' ' + 'Second'

    • Increment

      2++

    • Decrement

      2--

  2. Assignment Operators

    x = 10;
    x += 10;

  3. Comparison Operators

    • is Equal

      ==

    • Trippe Equal (also compares types)

      ===

    • Not Equal to

      10 != 30

    • Greater than

      20 > 10

    • Less than

      10 < 11
